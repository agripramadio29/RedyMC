About Us

It is a long established fact that this Motorcycle Club is the
best and most respected in Jakarta, especially in Dewi Sartika.

Best MC

Funnily enough, the name 'Redy MC' was not considered until Fikri 'Fren' A.P. mentioned it to Agri 'Condor' Pramadio, Ananda 'Esmun' E.J., Afryanto 'Soto Babat' R. and Aziz 'Nyaman' W.P. Back then this little group was called The Afry's. The turning point from which this little group transformed into the best MC was because of Mas Fajar 'OOP-God'. He incited our first touring, which will commence on 28th Sept 2024 to Bogor. Upon hearing this, Condor, Nyaman, Esmun and Soto Babat felt excited with several complications including the destination. Condor suggested Kuningan but not Kuningan in Central Java, but rather Kuningan City. Condor's statement quickly gained laughs and ridiculed by Nyaman, Soto Babat, Esmun and M. 'E-Money' Rohadi.
